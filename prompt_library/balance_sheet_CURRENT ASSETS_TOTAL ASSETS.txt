EXPLANATION OF TOTAL ASSETS: 
Total Assets 
• Definition: Represents the sum of all resources owned by a company, including both current and 
non-current assets, used to generate revenue and sustain operations. 
• Explanation: Total assets indicate a company's overall financial strength and capacity for growth. A 
higher total asset value suggests expansion, investment in infrastructure, and strong financial positioning, 
while a decline may signal asset depreciation, divestment, or financial stress. Balancing asset growth with 
revenue generation is crucial to maintaining efficiency and avoiding overinvestment or underutilization of 
resources. 
• Formula: 
Total Assets = Total Current Assets + Total Non-Current Assets 
• Example: 
Total Current Assets: ₹9,500,000 
Total Non-Current Assets: ₹10,500,000 
Total Assets = 9,500,000 + 10,500,000 = ₹20,000,000
